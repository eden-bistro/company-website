Industrial Information Technology

Our Industrial IT service helps manufacturers and industrial operators bridge the gap between operational technology (OT) and IT—bringing real-time visibility, automation and analytics to production and processes.

Key Capabilities

  • SCADA & PLC Integration : Real-time control and monitoring.
  • IoT & Sensor Networks : Data acquisition from field devices.
  • Edge Computing : Local processing for low-latency analytics.
  • Predictive Maintenance : Reduce downtime with condition-based alerts.
  • Protocol & System Integration : Modbus, OPC-UA, MQTT and other industrial protocols.

Benefits

By integrating sensors, controllers and analytics, we help you improve uptime, optimize throughput, reduce waste, and make data-driven operational decisions. Our solutions are built with security and regulatory compliance in mind.

Typical Technologies

We work with PLC vendors (Siemens, Schneider, Allen-Bradley), SCADA platforms, MQTT brokers, and analytics stacks. We also support integration with ERP and MES systems for end-to-end visibility.
