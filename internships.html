Internships & Mentorship

Our structured internships provide mentorship, hands-on experience, and the opportunity to work on real projects.

Program Structure

  • Duration: 3 – 6 months (project dependent)
  • Mentorship: One-on-one with instructors
  • Outcome: Portfolio-ready projects, certificate, and references

Eligibility

Open to motivated youth with a basic interest in electronics or ICT. Prior experience is helpful but not required.
